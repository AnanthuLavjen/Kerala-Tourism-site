Ernakulam

Ernakulam is the central business district of Kochi, Kerala, India. It's the eastern part of the mainland of Kochi city. The name "Ernakulam" comes from the name of the famous Ernakulathappan Temple, a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva

Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of Kerala state in southwestern India. It's located on the Arabian Sea, on the west coast of India, near the southern tip of the mainland.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is a district and city in the Indian state of Kerala. It's located on the Malabar Coast and is the fashion capital of Kerala.

Visit
Thrissur

Thrissur is a commercial and cultural center in Kerala, India. It's known as the cultural capital of Kerala and the land of Poorams. The city is home to over 10% of Kerala's population and is known for its ancient temples, churches, and mosques.

Idukki

Idukki is a district in the Indian state of Kerala. It is located in the Western Ghats in the southwest of the country. Idukki is the second largest district in Kerala by area but has the lowest population density.It is known for its dense forests and mountainous hills.

Kollam

Kollam is a district in Kerala, India. It is located on the southwest coast of the state, 70 kilometers north of Thiruvananthapuram, the state capital. The district is 2,491 square kilometers, making up 6.48% of the state's total area.

Alappuzha

Alappuzha is a district in Kerala, India, known for its beaches, boat races, and backwaters. It's also known as the "Venetian Capital" of Kerala and the "Venice of the East".


Visit
Kannur

Kannur is a coastal city in the north of Kerala, India. It's known for its beaches, natural beauty, folk art, and loom industries.
